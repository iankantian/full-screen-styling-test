The advantages of SVG: Any geometric graphic, is far more efficiently transmitted over the Internet as compressible strings, which are the nature of SVG.
Any scaling always results in sharp, highly defined edges without aliasing.
Some vector graphic work CAN be accomplished with text, especially the expansive Unicode and Emoji fonts. However, you cannot guarantee that a particular style or font somewhere won't knock your layout around. SVG has precise methods to control viewport, scaling and is completely controllable through css.
You could add an outline border to these icons or change the colors with a single line of css. Even today that's not going to happen in a browser using image manipulation
